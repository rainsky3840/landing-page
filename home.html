#Rain 3D Studio

About Me

Rainwind, low-poly 3D designer and aspiring coder

Focused on developing models and software that innovate user experience. Started 3D modeling in 2016 to make obj-format models for Minecraft mods. Specialty is military and science fiction aircraft with plans to cover ground vehicles as well. Modeling style is low-poly. The look and feel of the original is kept but with a reduced amount of polygons and pixels. Elements are simplified to cut down on production time, all while keeping accurate dimensions and shape. These measures enable a wider selection of content to be available for different mods.

MCHELI

MCHeli is a Japanese vehicle mod for 1.7.10 made by EMB4. It includes a vast selection of helicopters, planes, tanks, cars, and ground equipment. Development has ceased since 2016, but user-created addons are still being published.

Releases
Military Aircraft Addons : Cold War Pack / WW2 Aircraft Pack / Modern Aircraft Pack
Science Fiction Addons : Star Wars Pack / Clone Wars Pack
HMV2

HMV2 is a Japanese vehicle mod for 1.12.2 made by scope_mg36. It also supports user-created addons similar to MCHeli. Combined with sister mods like GVC (adds PVE with vehicles and mobs) and HMG2 (adds guns), it offers a total warfare experience similar to Flans mod.

Releases
Cold War Pack for HMV2
DS COMBAT

Diamond Star Combat is a vehicle mod in active development for 1.19.2 made by 1whohears. Primarily focused on air combat, with support for ground and naval vehicles. Unique aircraft physics introduce a learning curve while still making flying rewarding. Distinctive features include customizable loadout options, integrated HUD in cockpit, and different types of missiles.
